abstract: 'We aimed to identify diagnosis-specific/transdiagnostic/transoutcome multivariable
  candidate predictors (MCPs) of key outcomes in mental disorders. We conducted an
  umbrella review searching MEDLINE/Embase (19/07/2022), including systematic reviews
  of studies reporting on MCPs of response, remission, recovery, or relapse in DSM/ICD-defined
  mental disorders. We included 117 systematic reviews, 403 studies, 299,888 individuals
  with mental disorders, testing 796 prediction models. We identified numerous MCPs
  within disorders for response, remission and relapse, but none for recovery. Transdiagnostic
  MCPs of remission included lower disease-specific symptoms, female sex/higher education,
  and quality of life/functioning.'
